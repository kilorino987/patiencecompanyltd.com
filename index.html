About Us

Patience Company Limited is a privately owned company, which was started with an intention of making a difference in peoples’ lives through a supply of professionally qualified agency staff who are proficient, trustworthy and hardworking. We supply medical staff to work with patients in hospitals and care homes. We also supply placements in facilities such as: psychiatric units, learning difficulty centres and schools, as well as supported living accommodation.
